Safety

Child Safety Standards

BaatCheet, developed by IJ Roy, has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation, including CSAE and CSAM.

Strict prohibition

BaatCheet prohibits child sexual abuse material (CSAM), child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), grooming, sexual solicitation of minors, trafficking, sextortion, predatory behavior, and any content or conduct that exploits or endangers children.

Reporting contact

Report child safety concerns immediately to ijroy037@gmail.com. Include relevant usernames, account identifiers, screenshots, message context, and the reason for the report if it is safe to do so.

Moderation and escalation

Reports involving child safety are reviewed with priority. BaatCheet may restrict accounts, preserve relevant evidence, remove content where technically available, escalate serious threats, and cooperate with appropriate legal, platform, or child safety authorities when required.

User responsibility

Users must not share, request, store, encourage, or distribute exploitative content involving minors. Users must not use BaatCheet to contact minors in unsafe, sexual, coercive, deceptive, or exploitative ways.

Contact

For child safety standards, reports, or urgent safety concerns, email ijroy037@gmail.com.

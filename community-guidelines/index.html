Community Guidelines

BaatCheet is intended for safe, respectful direct messaging. These guidelines explain behavior that is not allowed.

Prohibited behavior

  • Harassment, bullying, threats, hate, intimidation, or repeated unwanted contact.
  • Abuse, exploitation, sexual exploitation, or unsafe behavior toward any person.
  • Illegal content, instructions for illegal activity, or content that promotes serious harm.
  • Impersonation, identity deception, phishing, scams, or misleading account behavior.
  • Spam, automation abuse, unsolicited promotional messaging, or attempts to disrupt the service.
  • Sharing private information without permission, including doxxing or account compromise attempts.

Messaging expectations

Users should communicate clearly, respect boundaries, and avoid sending unwanted, harmful, or deceptive messages. BaatCheet may restrict access or remove accounts when behavior violates these guidelines, law, safety expectations, or platform requirements.

Reporting

To report guideline violations, email ijroy037@gmail.com with relevant account details, screenshots if available, and a short description of the concern.

About BaatCheet

A public support site for a clean messaging app.

BaatCheet is developed by IJ Roy as a lightweight chat and direct messaging app. This website provides official public pages for account deletion, privacy practices, terms, community expectations, child safety standards, and support contact.

The site is static and hosted through GitHub Pages. Account deletion and support requests are handled manually through email at ijroy037@gmail.com.

App focus

Messaging essentials without unnecessary weight.

Direct chat

Simple one-to-one conversations with account identity and participation data managed by the app backend.

Security minded

Public policies describe authentication, session records, public keys, device identifiers, and push notification tokens.

Transparent pages

Play review pages are available without login, including privacy, terms, account deletion, and safety standards.

Email support

Deletion, privacy, support, and safety reports can be sent directly to IJ Roy by email.

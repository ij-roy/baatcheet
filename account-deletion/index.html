User account requests

Account Deletion

BaatCheet users can request account deletion by email. BaatCheet is developed by IJ Roy, and deletion requests are handled manually through ijroy037@gmail.com.

How to request deletion

  1. Email ijroy037@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: BaatCheet Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include your username and any account email or identifier used in the app.
  4. You may include a reason for deletion, but it is optional.

Data deleted where applicable

After verification, BaatCheet will delete or de-identify data tied to the account where applicable, including account profile, username/account record, authentication/session records, chat participation data tied to the account, FCM push token, Android device ID stored for notifications, public key, and locally associated account metadata on the server where applicable.

Data that may be retained

Data required for legal, security, abuse-prevention, dispute, or platform compliance may be retained for a limited period.

Some messages/content already delivered to other users or stored in conversation records may be retained where needed for conversation integrity, safety, or legal compliance.

Processing window

IJ Roy will acknowledge requests by email and process them within a reasonable period, defaulting to 30 days unless verification or legal retention requires more time.
